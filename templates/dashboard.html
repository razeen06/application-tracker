How to use Application Tracker

Add applications from the extension

Install the Intern Copilot Chrome extension. When you submit an application on a supported job board it'll offer to track it, or open the extension's popup and click "Track this page as an application" on any job posting.

Review and manage here

This dashboard lists everything you're tracking. Filter by status, or change one by hand any time with the dropdown on its row.

Connect Gmail for AI updates

Grant read-only Gmail access once, then click "Update Application Status" whenever you want it to scan recent mail and match updates to your applications.

Review AI suggestions

A matched email becomes a suggestion banner under that application, classified as Interview Offered, Action Required, Progress, or Rejected. Nothing is applied automatically.

Clear a suggestion

Click the banner to open the source email and clear its red marker -- the suggestion itself stays visible until resolved. Click "Accept" to apply it as-is, or just pick a different status from the dropdown yourself; either way resolves it.

Connect the extension

Click "Connect Extension" so the installed extension can sync using this account automatically, no copy-pasting a token.
